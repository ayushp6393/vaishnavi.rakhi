Your Trusted Wholesale Rakhi Partner

Welcome to Vaishnavi Rakhi – Kolkata's premier wholesale rakhi supplier, proudly serving retailers, distributors, and gifting businesses across India for over 15 years.

Nestled in the heart of Ram Rahim Market, we offer an unmatched collection of rakhis at the lowest prices in all of India. From traditional thread rakhis to designer sets, kids rakhis to premium bhaiya-bhabhi combos – we have it all under one roof.

Our mission is simple: bring you premium quality at wholesale prices so you can delight your customers while maximizing your margin. With fast pan-India delivery and flexible bulk deals, we are the partner every retailer deserves.
